Hamburger Recipe

Delicious and juicy hamburger recipe!

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat
  2. Form beef into patties
  3. Grill patties for 4-5 minutes per side
  4. Toast buns
  5. Assemble burger with toppings
  6. Serve with condiments
